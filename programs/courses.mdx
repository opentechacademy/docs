## Computer Hardware Path Courses

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="HW101: Hardware Fundamentals" icon="gauge" href="/programs/courses/hw101">
    *Beginner · 22 Hours*
    
    Learn what the main parts inside a computer do, how they connect, and how to recognize them.
  </Card>

  <Card title="HW201: CPU Deep Dive" icon="microchip" href="/programs/courses/hw201">
    *Intermediate · 12 Hours*
    
    Learn CPU ideas in plain English: cores/threads, clock speed, caching, thermal throttling, and troubleshooting.
  </Card>

  <Card title="HW202: Motherboard Deep Dive" icon="circuit-board" href="/programs/courses/hw202">
    *Intermediate · 12 Hours*
    
    Explore chipset lane allocations, firmware configurations (BIOS/UEFI), and no-POST diagnostics.
  </Card>

  <Card title="HW203: RAM Deep Dive" icon="memory" href="/programs/courses/hw203">
    *Intermediate · 11 Hours*
    
    Understand temporary workspaces, memory channel routing, timings, compatibility, and hardware stability testing.
  </Card>

  <Card title="HW204: Storage Deep Dive" icon="database" href="/programs/courses/hw204">
    *Intermediate · 11 Hours*
    
    Detail SATA and NVMe SSD interfaces, hard drives, data backups, cloning setups, and wear management.
  </Card>

  <Card title="HW205: Expansion, Power & Cooling" icon="fan" href="/programs/courses/hw205">
    *Intermediate · 11 Hours*
    
    Covers expansion cards, PSU rails and headroom, case airflow design, and upgrade validation.
  </Card>

  <Card title="HW206: Graphics Deep Dive" icon="gamepad" href="/programs/courses/hw206">
    *Intermediate · 13 Hours*
    
    Understand GPU pipelines, VRAM bandwidth, display connector standards, graphics drivers, and displays.
  </Card>

  <Card title="HW301: Protocols & Connectors" icon="cable" href="/programs/courses/hw301">
    *Advanced · 15 Hours*
    
    Learn internal buses, external interfaces, network protocols, cable limits, and connector pinouts.
  </Card>

  <Card title="HW401: Full System Integration" icon="laptop-code" href="/programs/courses/hw401">
    *Advanced · 18 Hours*
    
    Bring the hardware stack together: planning, compatibility, validation, and an autograded capstone system project.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
