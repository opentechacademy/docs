We are launching a new local community initiative: **Free Build-to-Own PC Workshops**. These events are designed to teach local students and hobbyists how computers work from the inside out, providing them with a finished computer they can use for school, coding, and creativity.

---

## Program Details

- **Location**: Prince William County or Manassas, Virginia.
- **Cost**: 100% free for all selected participants.
- **Goal**: Learn to source, assemble, configure, and troubleshoot a modern desktop computer.
- **Outcome**: Participants take home the computer they built, free of charge. (Note: to start, a temporary device may be awarded as we scale up.)

---

## Current Campaign & Fundraising Goal

Our fundraising goal is an ongoing, "as much as possible" target to launch and scale the program.

```mermaid
graph TD
    Donations(Community Donations) --> Goal(Ongoing Target: As much as possible)
    Goal --> |Purchase Parts| PCParts(PC Components: CPU, RAM, Storage, Case, Power Supply)
    Goal --> |Acquire Tools| Toolkit(Tools & Assembly Workspace Supplies)
    Goal --> |Organize Venue| Venue(PWC / Manassas Event Space)
    PCParts & Toolkit & Venue --> Workshop(In-person Workshop Launch)
    Workshop --> StudentPC(Student Keeps the PC / Temporary Device to Start)
```

**Where the funds will go:**
- **PC Components**: Sourcing reliable desktop hardware (CPUs, RAM, storage, motherboards, and power supplies). To start, a temporary device may be awarded.
- **Assembly Tools**: Screwdrivers, static bands, thermal paste, and monitoring displays.
- **Venue & Safety**: Booking accessible training spaces and securing insurance.
- **Student Supplies**: Training handbooks, quick-reference guides, and operating system licenses.
