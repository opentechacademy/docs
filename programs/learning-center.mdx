The **Open Tech Academy Learning Center** (available at [learn.opentechacademy.org](https://learn.opentechacademy.org)) is our central educational platform. It hosts interactive, self-paced courses designed to take learners from absolute zero to technical competency.

---

## Key Features

- **Free Enrollment**: Anyone can browse lessons and courses immediately without paywalls.
- **Account Progress**: Learners can create a free account to track their module progress, save quiz scores, and submit practical assignments.
- **Public Certificates**: Upon completing all course requirements, learners earn a downloadable certificate containing a unique verification code.

---

## Course Catalog

### Computer Hardware Fundamentals (Beta)
Our flagship course is currently in public beta. It covers the essential hardware components that power modern computers.

**Modules Include:**
- **Cables and Ports**: Understanding USB, HDMI, DisplayPort, Ethernet, and power cables.
- **Motherboards**: Identifying layout, PCIe expansion slots, power connectors, and BIOS/UEFI.
- **CPUs & Cooling**: Processor architecture, sockets, thermal paste, and heat sinks.
- **RAM & Storage**: Differences between DDR memory, SSDs (SATA and NVMe), and HDDs.
- **System Building Assembly**: Step-by-step checklist for building a functional desktop PC.
- **Troubleshooting Basics**: Diagnostic procedures for common POST errors and boot failures.

## Certificate Verification

To maintain the credibility of our credentials, we host a public verification service at [verify.opentechacademy.org](https://verify.opentechacademy.org).

Employers, teachers, and sponsors can verify any Open Tech Academy certificate by entering the unique verification code printed on it. The verification lookup queries our database and returns:
- Recipient Name
- Course Title
- Issue Date
- Expiration or Revocation status (if applicable)

<Info>
  The verification system is fully automated and runs serverless functions against our central PostgreSQL database to verify records in real time.
</Info>
