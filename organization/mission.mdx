**Open Tech Academy** is founded on the belief that access to technology education is a fundamental driver of opportunities. We design resources, courses, and software toolkits to empower learners around the globe, with a focus on absolute beginners.

---

## Our Mission

> We reduce barriers to technology education by publishing free, high-quality courses and resources that anyone can start using immediately—no expensive licenses, no gatekeeping.

We achieve this mission through:
- **Free self-paced courses** in our online Learning Center.
- **In-person build workshops** where participants learn computer engineering and keep their PCs.
- **Local browser utilities** to make common digital tasks easier and privacy-respecting.

---

## Core Values

### 1. Student-Led Leadership
Open Tech Academy is built and operated by student leaders, builders, and developers. This structure provides invaluable hands-on experience in software engineering, project management, and nonprofit governance.

### 2. Radical Accessibility
All of our content is available without sign-up blocks. You only need a free account in the Learning Center if you wish to track your progress and earn certificates. We never include ads, paywalls, or upsells.

### 3. Build in the Open
We open-source our tools and website platforms. Our codebases are designed to be remix-friendly, encouraging other schools and community groups to adapt and reuse our materials.

### 4. Direct Impact
We ensure that every dollar raised goes directly towards supporting educational resources, server infrastructure, and workshop equipment.
