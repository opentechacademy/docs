**Open Tech Academy** is a student-led IRS-recognized 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization dedicated to keeping technology education free and accessible for everyone. We believe in reducing barriers to entry by providing open-access resources, self-paced courses, and practical workshops with no paywalls or upsells.

<CardGroup cols={3}>
